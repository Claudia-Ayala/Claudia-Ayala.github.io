Info

STD is a sexual transmited disease caused by sexual contact which can cause infections.STD can also be spread by skin to skin or drug use.It also can be passed through breast feeding and unborn children.

-Chlamydia: is a very small parasitic bacterium.The medication is antibiotics.

-Gonorrhea: is caused by an infection with the bacteria Neisseria gonorrhoeae.Its spread through sexual contact with an infected person. This bacteria is treated with antibiotics such as ceftriaxone, azithromycin, and doxycycline.

-Trichomoniasis: is caused by parasite. To prevent it use condoms. The medication is antibiotics and the treatment involves both partners taking onee large dose of a specific oral antibiotic.

-Syphilis: is caused during a sexual intercourse.Antibiotics and Penicillin antibiotic are medications. Its treated with penicillin and also the sexual partner should be treated.
